ABOUT US BOOTHS

Enmanuel Mayo, the founder of US Booths and an upholstery expert is swaying the US furniture industry. With more than 16 years of experience decorating commercial and residential spaces, he is a crackerjack at designing world-class furniture that boosts a room’s functionality and value.

EXPERIENCE

As a dedicated, committed and skilled furniture designer, Mayo aspires to create a distinct identity in the market by offering supreme services through his US Booths venture. He uses his vision and expert insights to create designs that you love and your space demands to amplify your impact in the world.
